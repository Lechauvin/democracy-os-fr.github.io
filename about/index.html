Our mission


1. Support civic movements, institutions, start ups, collectives and any type of organization that is ready to embrace online participatory democracy by using a free, simple and powerful tool.
2. Gather citizens and create a community ready to adapt this tool to the constraints of our democracy.
3. Promote a culture of citizen participation of a new kind thanks to the Internet and foster the emergence of s Civic Tech ecosystem in France.
