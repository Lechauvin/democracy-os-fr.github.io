What is DemocracyOS?

DemocracyOS is an open source platform : it is free, independent and replicable.

It aims at fostering the participation of any citizen in lawmaking, at a time in history where the Internet has changed almost every aspect in our daily lives except the way our democracies work.

Started in 2012 by developers and political analysts in Argentina, it has since then spread in many places, United-States, Mexico, Spain... and now France.

Who's behind DemocracyOS France?

We are a group of French citizens who has been inspired by initiatives from DemocracyOS, and wanted to adapt it in order to update French democracy.

We set ourselves three objectives:



Support civic movements, institutions, political parties and any type of organization that is ready to embrace online participatory democracy by using a free, simple and powerful tool.


Create a community of citizens ready to adapt this tool to the constraints of our democracy.


Promote a culture of citizen participation of a new kind thanks to the Internet and foster the emergence of civic tech ecosystem in France.


We incorporated a non-profit corporation in early 2015 in order to structure our action in this regard.
